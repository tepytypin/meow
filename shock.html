SHOCK

Types of shock:
  • Hypovolemic shock
  • Obstructive shock
  • Distributive shock
  • Cardiogenic shock

Hypovolemic shock:

hypo - low, volemic - volume

Losing volume, duh. Volume drops means pressure drops.

Etiologies:
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Any forms of blood loss
    • GI bleed
    • Uterine bleed
    • AAA rupture

Obstructive shock:

Something must be obstructing the blood flow.

Etiologies:
  • Tension pneumothorax
  • Cardiac tamponnade
  • Pulmonary embolism
